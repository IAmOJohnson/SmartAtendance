📈 Semester Aggregate Attendance

Weighted attendance rate per student per course.
Students below the exam threshold cannot sit the exam.

How it's calculated:
Rate = (Present × P_weight + Late × L_weight) / (Total × P_weight) × 100
Default weights: Present = 1.0 pt  |  Late = 0.5 pt  |  Absent = 0 pts
Exam threshold: 75% (configurable per course via Admin → Courses → ⚙️ Settings)
