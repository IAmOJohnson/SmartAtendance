📖 How to Mark Attendance

  1. Click on "Attend Class" above
  2. Select an active class from your department
  3. Follow the verification method required:
    • 🔵 QR Only: Show your QR code to the lecturer's scanner
    • 🟢 QR + Face: Scan QR code, then verify with face recognition
    • 🔴 QR + Fingerprint: Scan QR code, then verify with fingerprint (unavailable)
  4. Make sure to mark both ENTRY and EXIT
⚠️ Important: You can only attend classes for courses in your department!
