⚙️ {{ course.course_code }} — Attendance Settings

These settings affect how attendance is weighted and what percentage a student needs to sit the exam for {{ course.course_title }}.

Formula:
Rate = (Present × P_weight + Late × L_weight) / (Total × P_weight) × 100

Example with defaults: 8 Present + 2 Late out of 15 sessions
→ (8×1 + 2×0.5) / (15×1) × 100 = 60%
Student needs this % attendance to sit the exam. Default: 75%
Default: 1.0
Default: 0.5 (half credit)

Absent always = 0 points. Incomplete sessions are excluded from aggregate calculation.
